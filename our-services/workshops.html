    Workshops

    SmartStart consultants conduct interactive workshops on the following topics:

    Taking your proposal from good to great:
    • The importance of logic model thinking
    • Your project is the bridge from problems to solutions
    • Describing the setting with visuals
    • Project goals: Moving from outputs to outcomes.
    • Describing project activities and participants with visuals
    • Broader impacts
    • Project sustainability
    • Whats hot with funders
    • Proposal checklist
    Evaluation Strategies:
    • Developing quantifiable program goals and guiding evaluation questions.
    • Developing a formative and summative evaluation plan tied to the goals, and guiding evaluation questions, and project activities.
    • Developing a logic model as a snapshot of your project.
    • Developing an evaluation timeline.
    • Developing benchmark/milestone charts to track progress.
    • Developing evaluation forms and survey instruments.
    • Posting forms online.
    • Basics of Excel and SPSS.
    • Presenting results including the basics of PowerPoint.
    • Reporting results - report format, graphs, and tables.
    • Integrating evaluation into the project.
    Developing Outcomes:
    • Aligned with ABET expectations and terminology (if applicable)
    • Step-by-step process to develop learning outcomes for each course
    • Outcome levels based on Bloom’s Taxonomy
    • Write student-focused, action-oriented, measurable learning outcomes
    • Vertically align course learning outcomes with pre- and post-requisite courses
    Informal Assessment Strategies:
    • Developing questions based on Bloom’s Taxonomy levels
    • Aligned with state content standards
    • Informal assessment activities
    • Graphic organizers
    • Performance assessment products
